About Vancoillie Studio.

Built on speed, security and simplicity. Premium digital experiences for freelancers and small businesses.

Hi, I'm Batiste.

I'm a web developer and app creator with a passion for fast, secure, and user-friendly digital experiences. With more than 3 years of experience, I've built websites, iOS apps, and software tools for freelancers and small businesses.

My philosophy is simple: technology should work for you, not the other way around. I build products that load fast, stay secure, and feel intuitive for end users.

I work from Roeselare and help businesses transform their online presence into a powerful tool for growth.
