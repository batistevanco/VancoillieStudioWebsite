Hosting, domain & SEO

Besides the website itself, I also take care of hosting, your domain name, basic SEO and Google Search Console. One point of contact so everything stays aligned.

You can choose how far I go in the technical setup: either I handle the full package including hosting and domain, or I build the website and deliver the static files so you (or your IT partner) can host everything yourself.

Option 1: Full service

  • You choose a website package (Starter or Most popular).
  • I register and configure your domain and hosting.
  • SEO basics and Google Search Console are set up for you.
  • You contact one person for changes, questions and follow-up.

Option 2: Self-hosted

  • You choose a website package, without hosting/domain add-on.
  • I deliver the optimised static files (HTML/CSS/JS) ready to upload.
  • You or your own provider take care of domain & hosting.
  • If needed, I can advise on basic setup or migration.
